Users can draw their own Google doodle.

The page features:
Screenshot of google logo.
Drawing tools: marker, paint brush, and spray can
Sizes of drawing tools: large, medium, small
Colors: black, blue, red, green, and yellow
Paint bucket tool: it fills in the last area you drew on. If you try to paint before drawing anything, a pop-up box will say, "Draw before you paint."
Eraser tool.
Refresh tool: clears your drawing and refreshes page
